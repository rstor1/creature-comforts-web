ABOUT

Ryan Storm is a former Special Effects Makeup Artist with experience in film and television, contributing to productions such as The Day After Tomorrow and 300. His full list of screen credits can be found on his IMDB Profile . Today, he continues to explore the craft as a personal creative pursuit. With a background in industrial design and special effects, each mask is approached as a character—developed from initial sketch to final paint with a focus on texture, realism, and expressive detail.
